Our Story

A Legacy of
Unforgettable Events

◆

Founded in 2005 by the Ansari family, Ayesha Banquet was born from a singular vision: to create a sanctuary where dreams take shape and celebrations transcend the ordinary. Nestled in the heart of Mumbai, our grand halls have witnessed thousands of love stories, milestones, and moments of pure joy.

We believe every event is a masterpiece in the making. Our dedicated team of curators, chefs, and designers collaborate tirelessly to transform your vision into a living, breathing celebration that guests will talk about for generations.
